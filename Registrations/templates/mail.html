IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS AND GUIDELINES
  • For the record to be valid, more than 90% of total participants have to solve successfully. Otherwise, it will become void even if the previous number is beaten.
  • Everyone have to attempt the record in the same 30 minutes window.
  • A participant can leave anytime after solving a cube.
  • Any form of written or printed aid is strictly against the spirit of the record. DO NOT carry any solution guide, printed algorithm or any form of aid to the event. 
  • Do Not touch anyone else's cube during the official attempt. You might advise but cannot provide physical or visual aid to a fellow participant.
  • The whole event will be recorded. Recordings and photographic evidence will be sent to Guinness World Records for verification.
