WORLD RECORD SOCIETY - BITS Pilani

GUTS TO DREAM BIG

On the occassion of 123rd birth anniversary of Shri G.D. Birla, founder of BITS Pilani, we are attempting a Guinness World Record of maximum number of people solving Rubik's cube at the same time here in pilani.Our target is 4000 people from BITS and BET.
Come and be a part of Guinness world record on 16th April, 2017.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q) Is there any registration charge?
A) No, registration is absolutely free.

Q) When is the event?
A) On 16 April (Sunday), 4:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.

Q) Where is the event?
A) Birla Gliding Club (Gliders).

Q) Where will we get 4000 people from?
A) 2000 from four schools under BET and 2000 from BITS campus.

Q) Do I have to buy a Rubik's Cube?
A) No, 3x3 Rubik's Cubes will be provided to everyone.

Q) What if I don't know how to solve a Rubik's Cube?
A) No problem. Everyone will be given printed algorithms. Also, teaching sessions will be held.

Q) What if I already know how to solve a Rubik's Cube?
A) Great! You can help others and be a part of teaching team.
