GUTS TO DREAM BIG

On the occassion of 123rd birth anniversary of Shri G.D. Birla, founder of BITS Pilani, we are attempting a Guinness World Record of maximum number of people solving Rubik's cube at the same time here in pilani.Our target is 4000 people from BITS and BET.
Come and be a part of Guinness world record on 16th April, 2017.
